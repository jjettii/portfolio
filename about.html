About Me

I've been making videos for over twenty years. Since going full-time in 2021 I've worked on over 150 projects. My inspiration comes from making narrative shorts and skateboarding videos. These days, I enjoy working with all sorts of clients — documentary, outdoor, sports, ecommerce and luxury.

I care a lot about finding each client's voice and making their story shine through.
